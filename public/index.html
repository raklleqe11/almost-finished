How to Spot a Tourist Trap Fish Restaurant in Saranda

The laminated menu with photos. The waiter outside calling you in. Here's what to walk past — and where to go instead. Open in Google Maps →

What time do you open — and when does the fish run out?

We open at 8:30 AM every day and close at 10:00 PM. Full selection available until around 11am. Come early for whole fish, come late for deals on the last pieces.

Do you sell fish ready for the grill?

Yes. We clean, gut and scale any fish you buy. Tell us it's for a BBQ and we'll prep it exactly how you need it. No extra charge.

Do you serve tourists visiting villas and hotels?

Absolutely — a big part of what we do. We serve everyone from local families to holiday guests. Same fish, same price, no tourist markup.

What's the difference between your fish and supermarket fish?

Age. Supermarket fish in Saranda is often 2–3 days old and sometimes imported. Our fish landed last night or this morning from local Ionian boats. Clear eye, sea-air smell — not fishy.

Can I call to check what's fresh before coming?

Yes. Call us at 069 896 7528 and we'll tell you exactly what came in that morning.
